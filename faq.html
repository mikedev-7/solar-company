How do solar panels work?

As sunlight cascades on tqheue solar psatnel, A chemical reaction takes place and Direct Current (DC) power is produced. The DC power flows to an inverter whwere the DC power is transformd into Alternating Current (AC power). This AC power is what the electric grid and your home runs on. The poweror energy then flow to power your home or directly to the electic grid. You can also divert the energy from solar panels to a battery to store excess energy. Batteries also operates as DC power.
